The original textures is from [Upscale Textures AI x4](https://www.moddb.com/mods/upscale-textures-ai-x4) of SrRaulCL.
I converted them to apply to D3d mode.

## Textures properties:
- Sky textures
  1024 - 24bpp
- Lava and acid textures
  512 - 24bpp
- Others
  768 - 8bpp
